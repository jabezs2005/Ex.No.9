## 🎯 Aim
To explore and understand the various prompting techniques used for generating videos through AI models. The objective is to demonstrate how different prompt structures (simple vs. detailed prompts) affect the quality, coherence, and style of generated videos.

---

## ⚙️ Procedure

1. Selected an AI video generation tool (Adobe Firefly).  
2. Created two types of prompts:
   - Simple prompt  
   - Detailed prompt  
3. Entered the prompts into the AI tool.  
4. Generated videos with a duration of 15 seconds.  
5. Observed the outputs based on:
   - Visual quality  
   - Lighting and effects  
   - Camera movement  
   - Realism and detail  
6. Compared both outputs and analyzed differences.  

---

## 📝 Prompts

### 🔹 Simple Prompt
A car driving on a mountain road during sunset.


---

### 🔹 Detailed Prompt
A cinematic 15-second video of a winding mountain road during sunset. A modern car drives smoothly along a curved road surrounded by tall mountains and lush greenery. The sky is filled with golden, orange, and pink hues as the sun sets behind the peaks. Soft sunlight reflects on the road, and light fog moves through the valleys. The camera starts with a wide aerial drone shot, slowly descending toward the road, then transitions into a tracking shot behind the car, and finally ends with a panoramic view of the mountains at sunset. Ultra-realistic, 4K resolution, cinematic lighting, smooth motion, depth of field, lens flare, volumetric fog, highly detailed textures.


---

## 🎥 Output

### 🔹 Output from Simple Prompt
- Basic scene generation  
- Limited environmental details  
- Minimal camera movement  
- Less realistic lighting  
- Lower cinematic quality  

---

### 🔹 Output from Detailed Prompt
- High-quality and realistic visuals  
- Smooth camera transitions (drone + tracking)  
- Rich lighting and sunset effects  
- Better depth, shadows, and reflections  
- Cinematic and professional output

## ✅ Conclusion
Prompt engineering is essential for effective AI video generation. Well-structured and detailed prompts provide better control over the output, resulting in higher-quality, more coherent, and visually appealing videos. This experiment highlights the importance of including scene details, camera movements, lighting, and style in prompts.

## 📊 Result
The experiment demonstrates that **detailed prompts produce significantly better video outputs** than simple prompts. Adding descriptive elements improves the overall quality and realism.
